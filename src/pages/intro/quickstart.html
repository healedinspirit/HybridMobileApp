Healed In Spirit

QUICK START

Our faith in Jesus can act like a shield "with which we can extinguish all the fiery darts of the wicked one". When we hold strong to faith in Jesus, the darts that the enemy fires at us get stopped and the fire on the darts gets extinguished.

But, what happens when we're not ready with our shield? We end up getting hit with the arrows and we are left with residual injuries in need of care and healing.

Thankfully, Jesus offers us the healing and cleansing that we need to mend injuries to our spirit on the inside. This walkthrough will take you through a series of prayers you can pray to access the healing that Jesus offers us.

When you're ready, scroll down to begin!

 HEAT

"Jesus, please apply your healing oil to any heat injuries."

"Please restore any injured areas."

 PUNCTURES

"Jesus, please remove all projectiles that have caused me injury."

"Please sterilize all wounds with your living water and healing oil."

"Please close all open wounds."

 ADHESIVES

"Jesus, please cut away anything that is an unhealthy rope or cord."

"Please remove any labels or curses with your fire."

 CALLUSES

"Jesus, please apply your healing oil to any callused areas."

 CUTS

"Jesus, please sterilize any cuts with your living water and healing oil."

"Please close any open wounds."

 POISON

"Jesus, please remove all poison from my spirit."

 COMBUSTIBLES

"Jesus, please apply your fire to all wood, hay or straw in my spirit."

"Please wash me with your living water."

 WASTE

"Jesus, please apply your cleansing fire to any mud or contaminants."

"Please wash me with your living water."
