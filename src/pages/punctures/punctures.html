Healed In Spirit

PUNCTURES

An injury resulting from a sharp object piercing or perforating an outer layer.
SYMPTOMS

The symptoms of a puncture wound can vary, depending on the object that caused the injury. But usually a person will experience an initial sharp pain. Then, depending on several factors, a person might experience prolonged pain and discomfort. But sometimes a person might not experience any pain whatsoever. Some puncture wounds are accompanied by a toxic substance which may cause additional symptoms.

TREATMENT

The treatment for a puncture wound involves three steps. First, the object is removed. Then any toxin or infection is cleaned and/or removed. Then finally the wound itself is closed. Allow Jesus to administer healing to you by asking this series of questions:

  1. Jesus, please remove all projectiles that have caused me injury.
  2. Please sterilize all wounds with your living water and healing oil.
  3. Please close all open wounds.
  4. What lie was that injury trying to tell me?
  5. What truth do you want to replace that lie with?

 DETAIL

But the common thing about all these is that they're used from a distance.

Different projectiles can affect us differently like their real world counterparts. Needles and darts are used as more of an nuisance and impediment. Medium sized projectiles are used to injure and slow it's target. And longer weapons like spears are meant to reach deep down. And some of these projectiles can be fired with other things like poison or fire to increase the injury they cause.
